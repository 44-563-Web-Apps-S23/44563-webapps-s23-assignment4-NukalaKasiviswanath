Kasi Viswanath snips a play

MANIKIN AND MINIKIN (A BISQUE-PLAY) by Alfred Kreymborg

SHE.

Manikin!

HE.

Minikin?

[Quietly..]

SHE.

That fool of a servants has done it again.

HE.

I should say, she's more than a fool.

[goes..]

SHE.

A meddlesome busybody----

HE.

A brittle-fingered noddy!

SHE.

Which way are you looking? What do you see?
